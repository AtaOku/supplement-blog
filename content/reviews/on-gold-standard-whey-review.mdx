## Overview

Optimum Nutrition Gold Standard Whey is one of the best-selling whey protein powders in the world, with over 30 years of brand history. It's the flagship product of Optimum Nutrition and a benchmark in the protein powder market.

## Nutrition Facts

Per serving (30.4g):

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 120 kcal |
| Protein | 24g |
| Fat | 1.5g |
| Carbohydrate | 3g |
| Sugar | 1g |

## Ingredient Analysis

ON Gold Standard uses a blend of whey protein isolate, whey protein concentrate, and whey peptides. With whey isolate listed first, it delivers high protein content with low fat and carbohydrates.

The protein blend provides a fast-absorbing amino acid profile ideal for post-workout recovery. Each serving contains 5.5g of BCAAs and 4g of glutamine.

## Taste and Mixability

With over 20 flavors available, there's something for everyone. Double Rich Chocolate and Vanilla Ice Cream are the most popular choices. Mixability is excellent — it blends smoothly with water or milk without clumps.

## Value Analysis

While it sits in the premium segment, the cost per gram of protein is competitive when you buy larger sizes. The 5lb tub offers the best value per serving.

## Who Is It For?

- Athletes looking for a reliable, quality protein
- Anyone without lactose sensitivity
- Those who want flavor variety
- People who prioritize brand trust and consistency

## Who Should Look Elsewhere?

- Lactose-intolerant individuals (consider whey isolate or vegan options)
- Strict budget shoppers (more affordable options exist)
- Those wanting zero artificial sweeteners

## The Bottom Line

ON Gold Standard earned its reputation for good reason. High quality, consistent production, and an extensive flavor lineup make it a reliable choice. It's not the cheapest option, but the quality justifies the premium for most users.
