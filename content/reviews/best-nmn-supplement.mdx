## Why NMN Is Trending

NMN (nicotinamide mononucleotide) is the direct precursor to NAD+ — a coenzyme critical for mitochondrial function, DNA repair, and cellular energy. NAD+ levels drop ~50% between ages 40-60, and NMN supplementation is one of the most promising ways to restore them.

The FDA's January 2026 decision to reclassify NMN as a supplement (after a period of uncertainty) triggered a market boom.

## How to Take NMN

- Protect from light and moisture
