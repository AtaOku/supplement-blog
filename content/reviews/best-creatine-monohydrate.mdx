## Why Creatine Monohydrate?

Creatine monohydrate is the most studied form of creatine — and the most effective. Other forms (HCL, buffered, ethyl ester) cost more but haven't been proven to work better. The science is clear: monohydrate is the gold standard.

## Usage Guide

Take 3-5g daily. Mix with water, juice, or your protein shake. Unflavored creatine has no taste, so it goes with anything. Timing doesn't matter much — consistency is what counts.

## Loading Phase: Necessary?

Optional. Loading (20g/day for 5-7 days) saturates stores faster, but daily 3-5g reaches the same levels within 3-4 weeks. Skip the loading and keep it simple.
