## Why CoQ10 Matters

Coenzyme Q10 is essential for ATP production in your mitochondria. It's also a potent antioxidant. After age 35, your body's natural production declines. If you take statins, supplementation is particularly important as these drugs significantly deplete CoQ10.

## Ubiquinol vs Ubiquinone: The Critical Choice

| Feature | Ubiquinol (Active) | Ubiquinone (Oxidized) |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Bioavailability | 3-8x higher | Standard |
| Body conversion | Ready to use | Conversion required |
| Best for | Ages 40+ | Under 35 |
| Price | Higher | Lower |

**Our recommendation**: If you're over 40 or feel fatigued, choose ubiquinol. If you're younger and healthy, ubiquinone works fine.
